# TapWash Pitch Deck Revamp (General Santos First)
## Corrected and Comprehensive Core Sections

## 1) Problem Statement

Laundry demand in General Santos City is growing, but the service experience is still highly fragmented and mostly manual.

For customers, the current process is inconvenient and uncertain:
- Discovery is scattered across word-of-mouth, Facebook pages, and chat threads.
- Price comparison is difficult because rates, add-ons, and delivery fees are inconsistent and often unclear before booking.
- Booking and follow-up are done through calls or messages, creating delays and miscommunication.
- Progress visibility is limited, so customers do not know whether laundry is accepted, in process, out for delivery, or delayed.

For laundry shops, operations are also inefficient:
- Order intake, updates, and rider coordination are handled through ad hoc messaging.
- Status tracking is inconsistent across staff, increasing risk of missed handoffs and service errors.
- Shops lack a standardized digital channel to attract and retain repeat customers.

The result is a trust and efficiency gap: customers lose time and confidence, while shops lose operational focus and growth opportunities.

TapWash addresses this by digitizing the full customer-to-shop workflow in one marketplace experience, starting with General Santos City.

## 2) Market Segment and Market Size

### Target Market Segments (General Santos Beachhead)
TapWash focuses on high-frequency laundry users with clear pain from time constraints and convenience needs:
- Busy professionals with limited time for drop-off/pickup
- Dual-income households prioritizing convenience and predictable service
- Students and boarders who need affordable, reliable, pickup-friendly laundry options

### Geographic Focus
General Santos City (1st class highly urbanized city and regional commercial center in SOCCSKSARGEN) is the launch market because:
- Dense urban activity supports pickup and delivery workflows
- The city has a large base of students, workers, and renters with recurring laundry demand
- A focused city launch improves operational quality and unit economics before regional scale

### Bottom-Up Beachhead Sizing (General Santos)
- Population (2024): 722,059
- Average household size (Philippines): 4.1
- Estimated households: 722,059 / 4.1 = 176,112

Serviceable adoption assumption for early digital laundry marketplace users:
- 10% to 15% of households
- Serviceable household range: 17,611 to 26,417 households

This is TapWash's immediate Serviceable Available Market (SAM) for initial penetration and validation in General Santos.

### Top-Down Expansion Context (Philippines)
Beyond the beachhead, the Philippine laundry care/services market is sizable and still largely offline and fragmented.
- Philippine laundry care market value (reference in existing deck): USD 1.59B (2023)
- Laundry services revenue projection (reference in existing deck): USD 88.2M (2024)

Implication:
- General Santos is the execution wedge.
- National market data supports scale potential once unit economics and retention are validated in the launch city.

## 3) Solution (Actual TapWash Product Features)

TapWash is a mobile-first laundry marketplace that connects customers, laundry shops, and delivery operations through a single, trackable workflow.

### Customer Experience (Implemented)
- Discover verified laundromats in one feed
- Search and compare shops by location, service details, and pricing cues
- Save favorite shops for faster rebooking
- Book services with configurable options and load details
- Manage delivery addresses within the app
- Checkout with voucher support and selected payment method
- Track orders through status milestones from acceptance to completion
- View live rider location updates during delivery-related stages
- Receive in-app and web push notifications for major order events

### Shop Operations (Implemented)
- Centralized order queue for incoming and active orders
- Structured status progression (accepted, washing, drying, ready, out for delivery, completed)
- Dispatch flow for assigning riders to active orders
- Basic dashboard visibility for operational and revenue monitoring

### Platform and Governance (Implemented)
- Admin portal for partner shop onboarding and verification controls
- Voucher and home-offer management tools for promotions
- Support ticket queue for issue handling
- Role-based access and policy-enforced data boundaries

### Important Accuracy Note
TapWash currently focuses on workflow digitization, transparency, and operations coordination.
- Not positioned as a smart-tagging system today
- Not positioned as a fully integrated end-to-end payment processor today

This keeps the product narrative credible and aligned with the real system state.

## 4) Value Proposition

TapWash creates value for all three sides of the marketplace.

### For Customers
- Convenience: one app flow from discovery to tracking instead of fragmented chats/calls
- Transparency: clearer status visibility and event-based progress updates
- Confidence: verified shop exposure, order milestones, and rider-location visibility
- Time savings: less coordination effort per laundry transaction

### For Laundry Shops
- Digital demand capture: improved discovery by customers searching in-app
- Operational clarity: standardized order states and dispatch coordination
- Better service consistency: fewer manual handoff gaps across staff and riders
- Platform leverage: promotion tools (offers/vouchers) to stimulate repeat orders

### For the TapWash Platform (and Local Ecosystem)
- Standardizes a traditionally manual service category into a repeatable digital workflow
- Improves traceability of order events for support and service quality monitoring
- Builds a scalable foundation for future monetization layers (advanced analytics, deeper payment integrations, premium partner tools)

### Core Positioning Statement
TapWash saves time for customers, improves operational flow for laundromats, and digitizes a fragmented local service into a trusted, transparent marketplace model.

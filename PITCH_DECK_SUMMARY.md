## 📊 NUMBERS & ASSUMPTIONS BUILT IN

### Unit Economics (Slide 5 + Appendix A)
- AOV: ₱650
- Commission: 12%
- Operating cost/order: ₱45
- Net margin/order: ₱33
- Breakeven: ~100 orders/day

### Traction Scenarios (Slide 11)
**Conservative (Safe Baseline):**
- Year 1: 15 shops, 500 customers, 800 orders, −₱818K loss
- Year 2: 45 shops, 3.5K customers, 45K orders, +₱2.4M EBITDA (profitable)
- Year 3: 120 shops, 12K customers, 180K orders, +₱12.5M EBITDA

**Base Case (Realistic High):**
- Year 1: 25 shops, 1.2K customers, 2.5K orders
- Year 2: 80 shops, 10K customers, 150K orders, +₱10.3M EBITDA
- Year 3: 200 shops, 35K customers, 600K orders, +₱44.5M EBITDA

**Stretch (Aggressive Growth):**
- Year 1: 35 shops, 2K customers, 5K orders
- Year 2: 150 shops, 25K customers, 400K orders, +₱29.4M EBITDA
- Year 3: 350 shops, 80K customers, 1.5M orders, +₱113M EBITDA

### Market TAM
- Total PH laundry market: ₱15B annually
- Addressable (urban, tech-forward): ₱500M–₱700M
- TapWash 3–5% capture thesis by Year 3
